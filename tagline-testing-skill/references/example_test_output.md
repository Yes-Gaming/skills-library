# Example Test Output

This is a sample of what a completed test report looks like.

## Test Configuration
- Personas tested: Casual Kasper (Recreational Spender)
- Rounds per persona: 10
- Options tested:
  - Option A: Yes.com - Play Made Easy
  - Option B: Yes.com - Casino and Sports Made Easy
  - Option C: Yes.com - The Game is On
- Date conducted: November 2, 2025

## Quantitative Results

### Overall Preference (Q1)
Casual Kasper:
- Option A (Play Made Easy): 7/10 (70%)
- Option B (Casino and Sports Made Easy): 2/10 (20%)
- Option C (The Game is On): 1/10 (10%)

### Trust/Quality Perception (Q4)
Casual Kasper:
- Option A: 8/10 (80%)
- Option B: 2/10 (20%)
- Option C: 0/10 (0%)

### Order Bias Analysis
No significant order bias detected. Option A won regardless of presentation position.

## Qualitative Insights

### Why "Play Made Easy" Won

**Key Themes:**
1. **Clarity & Directness** - "It tells me exactly what I want to hear"
2. **Simplicity Promise** - "Makes it clear there won't be hassle"
3. **Memorability** - "Clean, punchy, sticks in your head"
4. **Alignment with Values** - "Speed and simplicity win every time"

**Representative Quotes:**
- "It promises what I actually care about: making my experience easy"
- "Clean, direct, gets straight to the point"
- "Shows they mean it by keeping the tagline simple too"

### Why Others Lost

**Option B (Casino and Sports Made Easy):**
- "Too wordy"
- "Trying to cram too much information into one line"
- "Clunky"
- "Don't need you to list what you offer in the tagline"

**Option C (The Game is On):**
- "Vague - doesn't tell me anything"
- "Trying too hard to sound catchy"
- "Sounds like something from a commentator"
- "Confusing - what game?"

## Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation
**Use "Play Made Easy"**

This tagline achieved 70% preference rate and 80% trust rating with strong, consistent reasoning across all rounds. It directly addresses the persona's core motivation (ease of use) and avoids the pitfalls of being too wordy or too vague.

### Key Strengths
- Clear value proposition
- Memorable and concise
- Builds trust while driving preference
- Aligns with brand promise of simplicity

### Why Not the Others
- Option B dilutes the message and feels corporate
- Option C fails to communicate concrete value

### Follow-up Testing Needed
Test Danish translation "Leg gjort nemt" with same methodology to ensure translation maintains appeal.
