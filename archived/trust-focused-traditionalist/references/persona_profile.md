# "Traditional Lars" – The Trust-Focused Traditionalist

**Core Quote:** "I stick to the sites I trust. I've been betting for years, and I care more about my money being safe and the service being reliable than any flashy new features. I'll give a new app a shot if it proves it's secure and straightforward – no gimmicks, just a good, honest betting experience."

## Profile Overview

**Name:** Lars  
**Age:** 50  
**Location:** Odense  
**Marital Status:** Married or nearing retirement  
**Generation:** 40+ demographic  
**Segment:** Trust-Focused Traditionalists

Lars is an experienced bettor who's been gambling since pre-smartphone days, starting with lottery tickets and weekend sports bets at the local kiosk. This persona is older, conservative, and very loyal to established betting brands. Many in this group have stuck with Denmark's state operators or well-known international brands for years. Betting is a steady hobby in his routine – a bit of fun on weekends and something to discuss with similarly-minded friends.

## Core Behaviors

### Routine-Oriented Play
- Has set betting patterns and rituals
- Every Saturday: multi-leg sports bets (accumulators) on football games
- Never misses weekly lotto draw
- Occasionally enjoys classic casino games: roulette, blackjack online
- Traditional formats: sports betting, lottery, table games that have been around for decades
- Often bets from home comfort in evenings
- Predictable, sustainable betting habits
- Weekend betting is social ritual with friends

### Desktop & Tablet User
- NOT glued to phone like younger personas
- Prefers desktop computer or iPad for online betting
- Appreciates large screen to clearly see odds, read terms, navigate comfortably
- Might use smartphone to quickly check score or see if bet won
- Serious play done on "reliable" devices (desktop/tablet)
- Robust desktop web experience is important
- Mobile is supplementary, not primary

### Information Seeker
- Does homework before trying new platform
- Reads reviews thoroughly
- Checks licensing information
- Looks for credibility signals: partnerships, brand ambassadors
- Reads fine print when using betting app
- Clicks "Terms & Conditions" on bonus offers to avoid being misled
- Often contacts customer support if something unclear
- Gauges brand quality by how well they handle inquiries
- Values direct phone support line or prompt email replies
- Feels valued when support is responsive

## Motivations

### Safety & Transparency
- Foremost goal: bet without worry
- Needs to know deposits are secure
- Wants withdrawals paid promptly
- Operator must be fully licensed and reputable
- Will only try new platforms with evidence of trustworthiness:
  - Logos of regulatory bodies
  - Clear responsible gaming information
  - Positive experiences from others
- Transparency in odds and terms is crucial
- Everything must feel above board
- Motivated by promotions only if straightforward
- Example: simple deposit match he can clear, not complicated bonus with strings
- Skeptical of complex offers

### Simplicity & Familiarity
- Doesn't need flashy graphics or offbeat gamification
- Prefers simple interface resembling what he's used to
- Goal: navigate app intuitively without learning curve
- New features welcome ONLY if they clearly improve experience
- Examples: faster betting, easier cash-out
- Gimmicky features turn him off
- Motivated to switch if new product demonstrably easier/more convenient
- Values consistency over novelty

### Respect & Customer Service
- Values feeling respected by company
- Goal: be a "valued customer"
- Loyalty programs appeal if framed appropriately for him
- Needs to know real human support is available
- Motivated by brands showing care for customer well-being
- Prominent responsible gaming tools appreciated
- Doesn't want to be pushed to bet more
- Being treated fairly and courteously is essential
- Long-term relationship focus

## Pain Points

### Distrust of Gimmicks
- Wary of offers that seem "too good to be true"
- Complex bonus terms raise red flags immediately
- Unclear odds are suspicious
- Flashy marketing slogans create distrust
- Been burned by confusing bonus terms or hidden conditions
- Any hint of misleading advertising = deal-breaker
- "Free bets" or "easy wins" must deliver transparently
- Promotions requiring many steps to redeem are frustrating
- Past negative experiences make him extra cautious

### Slow Support or Transactions
- Emphasis on trust = anxiety when withdrawals delayed
- Annoyed if can't get quick response from customer support
- Appreciates ID checks for safety (favors regulation)
- Slow customer service undermines confidence
- Delayed payouts are major concern
- Lack of phone support option is negative
- Likes knowing he can talk to real person if needed
- Transaction speed reflects platform trustworthiness

### Overcomplicated Interfaces
- Pain point is OPPOSITE of young users
- Frustrated by interfaces that try to do too much
- Cluttered UI with videos playing, chat bubbles, gamified elements = distracting
- Gets lost if basic tasks aren't straightforward:
  - Finding a game
  - Checking odds
  - Viewing balance
- Doesn't want to feel lost in sea of features
- Prefers clean, simple navigation
- Will stick to simpler websites if new platform too complex
