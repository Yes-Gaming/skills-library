## PHASE 2B: TOP 3 FINALISTS COMPARISON

**Context:** These are the top 3 logo finalists for Yes Games. Compare them carefully.

**Logos Presented (randomized order for this respondent):**

**Logo A:** Bold "YES" with shadow effect
**Logo B:** Bold "YES" in tilted rectangular stamp/sticker style
**Logo C:** Lowercase "yes" refined, clean, modern

---

## Test Responses

### Q1: Which of these three is your clear winner? Why?

Logo B! YES! Literally!

It's got that energy! The tilt makes it feel alive and exciting! Like it's shouting "YEAH, LET'S GO!" which is exactly the vibe I want when I'm opening up a casino app after a crazy shift at the club! The stamp thing is so cool - it's like someone just slapped that sticker on there like "BOOM, YES!" Super playful and fun!

Logo A is alright but kinda basic. Logo C is way too quiet and minimal - it's like the opposite of exciting. When I'm buzzing after work and want some thrill, I don't want minimalist design, I want ENERGY! Logo B delivers!

### Q2: Which feels most trustworthy?

Hmm, probably Logo C for trustworthy? It's the most professional and clean looking.

But honestly, I don't really care that much about "trustworthy" in the sense of serious and boring. I trust a brand that looks fun and doesn't take itself too seriously! Logo B feels trustworthy in a different way - like it's honest about being fun and exciting! It's not pretending to be some stuffy corporate thing. It knows it's a gambling app and it owns it!

So yeah, Logo C if you mean "bank-level trust," but Logo B if you mean "trust that this will actually be fun."

### Q3: Which feels most modern and premium?

Logo C is definitely more "premium" in that Apple, minimalist, expensive way. Super clean, refined, modern tech aesthetic.

But is premium what we want for a casino app?! I don't know! When I think premium, I think of boring business apps or expensive stuff I can't afford. Logo B feels modern in a more exciting way - like streetwear or sneaker culture or gaming aesthetics. That stamp vibe is trendy right now!

Logo A with the shadow feels a bit dated, like mid-2010s design. Logo C is modern-minimalist. Logo B is modern-energetic. I prefer the energetic version!

### Q4: Which fits iGaming/betting best?

Oh, 100% Logo B! No question!

It's got that playful gambling energy! The tilt, the stamp, the boldness - it all screams "FUN! GAMES! LET'S PLAY!" which is exactly what I want! When I open a casino app at 3am after closing the bar, I want something that matches my energy level! I'm buzzed, I'm excited, I want thrills!

Logo C is too calm for gambling. It's like decaf coffee. Sure, it's nice and refined, but where's the kick?! Where's the excitement?! Gambling should feel exciting!

Logo A is closer with the shadow and boldness, but it's still kinda standard. Logo B is the one that actually makes me feel like "YEAH, LET'S DO THIS!"

### Q5: Rank all 3 (1st, 2nd, 3rd) with reasoning

**1st Place: Logo B (Bold "YES" tilted stamp)**

This is IT! This is the winner! It's got everything - energy, playfulness, excitement, personality! The tilt makes it dynamic like it's actually moving! The stamp effect is so cool and different! The bold "YES" is literally saying YES to fun!

When I'm opening this app after a wild night at the club, this is the logo that matches my energy! It doesn't try to be serious or corporate - it knows it's entertainment and it OWNS it! It's confident, it's bold, it's fun! That's what gambling apps should be! The whole point is entertainment and thrill, and this logo captures that perfectly!

Also, it would look amazing as an app icon on my phone! Super recognizable, super eye-catching! My coworkers would see it and be like "Yo, what's that?" and I'd be like "It's this awesome casino app!" The logo creates conversation!

**2nd Place: Logo A (Bold "YES" with shadow)**

This one's good! It's got energy with the bold text and the shadow adds some depth which is cool! It's definitely gambling-appropriate - bold, strong, present. I like it!

But compared to Logo B, it feels a bit safe and standard. Like it's playing it cool instead of going all-in. The shadow is nice but it doesn't have the same personality as the tilted stamp. It's like the difference between a good night and a GREAT night - Logo A is good, Logo B is great!

Still, if Logo B didn't exist, I'd be totally happy with Logo A. It works, it's got presence, it's solid. Just not as exciting as Logo B.

**3rd Place: Logo C (Lowercase "yes" refined)**

Look, I get it. It's clean, it's modern, it's sophisticated. For a banking app or a meditation app or whatever, this would be perfect. But for a CASINO APP?! Come on!

Where's the excitement?! Where's the energy?! The lowercase makes it feel too quiet and gentle. "yes" vs "YES" - totally different vibes! When I'm gambling, I want to feel pumped up, not calm and refined!

It's not BAD - it's actually really well designed. But it's wrong for the purpose. It's like wearing a tuxedo to a nightclub. Sure, it's nice, but you're missing the point. Gambling is about thrill and excitement, and this logo doesn't deliver that feeling at all.

I appreciate the craftsmanship, but it doesn't make me want to play. And that's the whole point, right?!

### Q6: What makes your #1 better than your #2?

PERSONALITY! Logo B has SO much more personality than Logo A!

Logo A is like "Yes, here's a gambling app." Logo B is like "YES!! LET'S PARTY!!" The tilt creates this sense of motion and energy that Logo A's static shadow just doesn't match! It feels ALIVE!

Also, Logo B takes a risk with the design - it's bold, it's different, it doesn't play it safe. That's exactly the kind of attitude I want from a gambling brand! Gambling is about taking chances, having fun, being a bit wild! Logo B embodies that spirit! Logo A is more careful and standard.

The stamp effect is also way more interesting visually than a shadow. Shadows are everywhere - every logo has had a shadow at some point. But the stamp thing? That's unique! That's memorable! That makes it stand out!

When I'm scrolling through apps or seeing ads, Logo B would catch my eye instantly! Logo A would blend in more. In a crowded market, you want to POP, and Logo B definitely pops more!

Plus, Logo B would be amazing on merch! Imagine that stamp logo on a t-shirt or a sticker pack! It's got that streetwear, sticker-bombing vibe that's super trendy! Logo A is less versatile for that kind of fun brand extension.

## Key Takeaways (Interviewer Notes)

- Strong preference for high-energy, playful design
- Values excitement and personality over refinement
- Connects logo energy to personal energy level and use context (late night, post-work)
- Sees restraint as inappropriate for entertainment/gambling category
- Would engage with and talk about more distinctive branding
- Young, trend-aware perspective values boldness and risk-taking

**Winner:** Logo B (Bold "YES" tilted stamp)
**Primary Reason:** Maximum energy, personality, excitement, and fun factor that matches gambling entertainment purpose
