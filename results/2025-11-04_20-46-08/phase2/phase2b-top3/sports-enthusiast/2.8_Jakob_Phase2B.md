# Phase 2B Logo Testing - Top 3 Finalists Comparison

**Test ID:** PHASE2B-018 | **Date:** November 5, 2025 | **Phase:** 2B

## Respondent: Jakob (2.8 - Social/Influenced)
Age 28 | Marketing manager | Social betting 400-700 DKK/month

**Logos:** A: Lowercase "yes" | B: Bold shadow "YES" | C: Tilted stamp "YES"

## Responses

### Q1: Winner?
I think Logo C would be most popular with my friend group! The tilted stamp thing is trendy right now - everyone's into that streetwear, sticker aesthetic.

When we share our bet slips on our group chat, Logo C would look coolest. It's got personality and would make good content for Instagram stories.

Logo A is too corporate. Logo B is solid but forgettable.

### Q2: Trustworthy?
Everyone I know would probably say Logo A looks most legit and professional.

But Logo C has a different kind of trust - like indie brand trust. My friends would see it as authentic and different from corporate betting sites.

### Q3: Modern/Premium?
Logo A is "premium" in that Apple way. Logo C is "premium" in that Supreme/streetwear way.

Most people in my circle would find Logo C more interesting. The tilt and stamp effect are on-trend.

### Q4: Fits iGaming?
Logo B fits what people EXPECT from betting. Logo C fits what's actually COOL right now.

My friends would be more likely to try something that looks like Logo C - it feels fresh and different.

### Q5: Rankings
1st: Logo C - Most shareable, trendy, would generate conversation
2nd: Logo A - Looks professional, safe choice
3rd: Logo B - Standard, nothing special

### Q6: #1 vs #2?
Logo C would get people talking. When I show my friends a new app, I want it to be something interesting. Logo C is interesting. Logo A is just... nice. That's not enough to create social buzz.

**Winner:** Logo C (Tilted stamp) - Most trendy and shareable
