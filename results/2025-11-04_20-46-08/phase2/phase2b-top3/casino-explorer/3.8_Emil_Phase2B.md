**Test ID:** PHASE2B-028 | **Date:** November 5, 2025 | **Phase:** 2B

## Respondent: Emil (3.8 - Social/Influenced)
Age 23 | Influencer marketing | Trending games 200-400 DKK/week

**Logos:** A: Bold shadow "YES" | B: Lowercase "yes" | C: Tilted stamp "YES"

## Responses

### Q1: Winner?
Everyone's into that clean minimal vibe right now, so Logo B would probably be most popular with people I know.

All the trending apps have moved toward refined, simple logos. Logo B fits that aesthetic perfectly. My friends would see it and think "oh, this is one of those well-designed modern apps."

Logo C is cool and quirky but might be too niche. Logo A feels kind of standard.

### Q2: Trustworthy?
People I know would say Logo B looks most legit. Clean design = trusted brand in most people's minds.

The influencers I work with all prefer that minimal professional aesthetic. Logo B would get the social validation.

### Q3: Modern/Premium?
Logo B is definitely trending right now. That refined lowercase thing is EVERYWHERE on social media.

People associate that clean minimal look with premium brands. Logo B would perform well in social contexts.

### Q4: Fits iGaming?
Most people would say Logo A fits traditional gambling, but Logo B fits what's actually cool and shareable right now.

When games go viral on TikTok, they usually have clean modern branding like Logo B.

### Q5: Rankings
1st: Logo B - Most socially validated, trending aesthetic
2nd: Logo C - Some people would love the quirky vibe
3rd: Logo A - Standard, forgettable

### Q6: #1 vs #2?
Logo B has broader social appeal. Logo C would be polarizing - some love it, some hate it. For social recommendation, Logo B is the safer bet that most people would approve of.

**Winner:** Logo B (Lowercase "yes") - Social validation winner
