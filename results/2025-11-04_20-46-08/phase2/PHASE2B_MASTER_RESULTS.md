## KEY INSIGHTS & PATTERNS

### 1. DEMOGRAPHIC POLARIZATION

**Young/Modern Demographics (Casual, Casino, Cross-Entertainment):**
- Overwhelmingly prefer Logo 2 (88% win rate in these demographics)
- Value: clean, modern, versatile, minimal, mobile-optimized
- Reject: Logo 4 seen as dated/heavy, Logo 1 seen as gimmicky

**Trust-Seeker Demographic (40+ age group):**
- Overwhelmingly prefer Logo 4 (90% win rate)
- Value: dimensional depth = establishment, shadow = substance, authority
- Reject: Logo 2 seen as too light/insubstantial for financial transactions

**Sports Enthusiasts (Mixed age group):**
- Split evenly (Logo 4: 50%, Logo 2: 40%, Logo 1: 10%)
- Traditional sports bettors → Logo 4
- Modern/analytical/skeptical → Logo 2
- Social/trendy → Logo 1

### 2. DESIGN ELEMENT RECEPTION

**Shadow Effect (Logo 4):**
- Trust-Seekers: "dimensional credibility," "establishment signals," "substance"
- Others: "dated," "2010s design," "unnecessary," "heavy"

**Lowercase Typography (Logo 2):**
- Modern users: "approachable," "contemporary," "sophisticated," "premium"
- Trust-Seekers: "too light," "insubstantial," "lacks authority"

**Tilted Stamp (Logo 1):**
- Almost universally rejected (6% overall win rate)
- Creative/enthusiastic personas: "personality," "energy," "fun"
- Most others: "gimmicky," "hard to read," "unstable," "temporary"

### 3. CATEGORY FIT DIVERGENCE

**Traditional iGaming Aesthetic:**
- Logo 4 wins (48% say it fits iGaming best)
- Bold, shadowed, authoritative = traditional gambling look

**Modern Unified Platform:**
- Logo 2 wins for cross-product versatility
- Clean, adaptable = works across sports, casino, esports

**Key Strategic Question:** Should Yes Games look like traditional iGaming or modern entertainment platform?

### 4. USE CASE PATTERNS

**Mobile-First Users:**
- Strong preference for Logo 2 (clean, scalable, readable)
- Logo 4's shadow "gets muddy at small sizes"
- Logo 1's tilt "creates layout issues"

**Multi-Product Users:**
- Unanimous preference for Logo 2 (Cross-Entertainment: 10/10)
- Need versatility across sports, casino, esports
- Logo 4 and 1 seen as "too category-specific"

**Traditional Gamblers (40+):**
- Strong preference for Logo 4
- Want visual indicators of trust and establishment
- Shadow effect = "solid and reliable"
