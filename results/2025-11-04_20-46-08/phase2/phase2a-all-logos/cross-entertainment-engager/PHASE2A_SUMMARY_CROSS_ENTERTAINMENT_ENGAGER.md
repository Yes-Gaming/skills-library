**Date:** November 5, 2025
**Persona Group:** Cross-Entertainment Engager (Variations 5.1-5.10)
**Phase:** 2A - All 7 Logos Comparative Testing

---

## Executive Summary

The Cross-Entertainment Engager persona group shows **strong consensus favoring Logo 2 (Refined lowercase)** for unified platform branding. This group values **cross-vertical consistency** and **functional integration** above all else, making them particularly sensitive to logos that work seamlessly across sports betting, casino gaming, and esports contexts.

**Key Finding:** Logo 2 wins on trust, integration, and overall preference. Logo 4 wins on visibility and social appeal but raises concerns about implementation consistency.

## Key Insights by Value Driver

### Integration Consistency (Primary Value)
**100% of respondents** evaluated logos through cross-vertical integration lens. Key concerns:
- Sports betting interface compatibility
- Casino game lobby integration
- Esports content contexts
- Multi-device consistency

**Winner:** Logo 2 - "neutral enough to work everywhere without clashing"

### Trust & Professionalism (Critical Value)
**100% consensus** that Logo 2 projects highest trust for financial transactions across multiple entertainment types.

Key trust signals identified:
- Professional restraint (not overselling)
- Consistent execution (suggests operational competence)
- Visual clarity (transparent, not manipulative)

### Scalability & Implementation (Technical Value)
High awareness of technical requirements:
- Small screen legibility (mobile icons, notifications)
- Background versatility (light/dark modes, varied UI contexts)
- Loading efficiency (simple shapes = fast rendering)
- UI integration (doesn't dominate interface elements)

**Winner:** Logo 2 - universally praised for technical implementation strength

### Social Shareability (Marketing Value)
Split between Logo 2 (trust/consistency) and Logo 4 (visibility/impact):
- Content creators (Alexander) and social media professionals (Mads) prefer Logo 4
- Others recognize Logo 4's marketing strength but prioritize sustained usability

**Winner:** Logo 4 for acquisition, Logo 2 for retention

## Persona Group Characteristics

### Unified Experience Expectations
This group expects:
1. **Seamless transitions** between sports betting, casino, and esports
2. **Consistent branding** across all entertainment verticals
3. **Professional integration** not "bolted together" products
4. **Multi-device sync** with consistent visual identity

### Skepticism About Integration Claims
Multiple respondents expressed doubt about unified platform delivery:
- "Most platforms fail at integration" (Nanna)
- "Should feel cohesive, not Frankenstein app" (Daniel)
- "Worried unified platform might be too complex" (Julie)

**Logo 2 wins by NOT overselling** - restrained design suggests realistic competence.

### Value Over Flash
80% prioritized long-term usability over short-term visibility:
- "Does it work?" over "Does it stand out?"
- Consistent performance across contexts over single-metric optimization
- Implementation feasibility over aesthetic boldness
