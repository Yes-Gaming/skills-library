"Oh, I really like Logo 2! It's clean and modern, but also looks trustworthy - and that's so important! I love the fun of gambling, but I also need to know that I'm on a safe platform. Logo 2 looks like a company that would have proper limits and responsible gambling tools. It's professional but not cold - there's something approachable about it. It says 'we're serious about keeping you safe while you have fun.' That's exactly what I need!"

**Key Themes:**
- Balance of fun and safety

- Approachable yet serious

### Q2: Trust Leader (Forced Choice)
**Question:** Which one feels most trustworthy for a betting/gambling platform?

**Kirsten's Response:**
"Logo 2, absolutely! Trust is key when you're gambling - I need to know the site is licensed and has good responsible gambling practices. Logo 2 looks like a professional company that would have clear limits I can set, warnings when I'm spending too much, and proper Spillemyndigheden licensing visible.

Logo 6 and Logo 3 are pretty, but they look too casual - like they're more about fun than safety. That worries me because gambling should be fun, but SAFE fun! Logo 4 looks aggressive - makes me think they just want my money. Logo 1 is too gimmicky.

Logo 2 strikes the perfect balance - it looks professional enough to trust, but not so corporate that it feels cold. I'd trust them with my weekend entertainment budget because they look like they'd help me stay in control."

**Trust Elements Identified:**
- Licensing visibility expectations
- Responsible gambling tools
- Spending limits and warnings
- Professional = protective
- Balance of fun and safety
- Control support systems

**Red Flags:**
- Too casual = safety de-prioritized
- Aggressive = exploitative
- Gimmicky = untrustworthy

### Q7: Immediate Eliminations (Open)
**Question:** Which logos would you eliminate immediately? Why?

**Kirsten's Response:**
"I'd eliminate four right away:

**Logo 3 and Logo 6 - Too casual!**
They're pretty, but they look too much like party invitations! Gambling is fun, yes, but it's also serious because it involves my money. These logos make it look too carefree and casual. I worry that a platform with these logos wouldn't take responsible gambling seriously. Where are the safety tools? Where's the professionalism? I need to see that they care about player protection, and these logos don't show that. Eliminated!

**Logo 4 - Too aggressive!**
This one makes me uncomfortable. The bold shadow feels pushy, like they're trying too hard to get my attention - and my money! It doesn't look like a platform that would help me set limits or warn me if I'm spending too much. It looks like they just want me to play more and more. That's not the kind of gambling environment I want. Trust is key, and this doesn't build trust! Eliminated!

**Logo 1 - Too gimmicky!**
The tilted stamp thing feels like a gimmick. Why is it tilted? It makes me question if other things about the platform are also 'off' or unreliable. I need straightforward, trustworthy platforms. Gimmicks make me nervous - if they're trying this hard to be different, what are they hiding? Eliminated!

Only Logo 2, 5, and 7 look like platforms that would have proper licensing and responsible gambling features. The others look fun, but not SAFE fun - and safety is non-negotiable for me!"

**Elimination Criteria:**
- Too casual = safety de-prioritized
- Aggressive = exploitative intent
- Gimmicky = unreliable
- Must demonstrate protection commitment
- Fun must be paired with safety
