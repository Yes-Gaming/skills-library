Strategic Recommendations

Based on 50 independent tests across 5 distinct user personas:

  1. Logo 2 emerges as the trust and quality leader - highest scores for trust (4.1/5) and modernity (4.9/5), with the only significant premium perception
  2. Logo 1 is the memorability champion - 58% recall rate, distinctive angle creates strong recognition value and communicates energy
  3. Logo 4 fits iGaming category best (4.1/5) but needs modernization - removing the dated shadow effect could significantly improve performance
  4. Logos 3 and 6 (script variations) show universally poor category fit with strong budget perception - should be eliminated from consideration
  5. Logo 7 creates cognitive friction despite modern aesthetic - abstraction hurts brand clarity more than it helps differentiation
  6. Logo 5 maximizes visibility through aggressive styling but sacrifices sophistication - functional but unsophisticated

Strategic Tension Identified

The data reveals a key decision point between:

  • Trust & Sophistication (Logo 2) vs. Memorability & Energy (Logo 1)
  • Category Fit (Logo 4) vs. Modern Design (Logo 2)

Next Phase Recommendation: Head-to-head testing of top 3 performers (Logos 1, 2, and 4) with simultaneous presentation to force direct comparison and preference ranking. This will help resolve the strategic tension and identify the optimal balance of attributes for the target market.

Testing Methodology

Phase 1: Sequential Individual Testing

Approach: Each of 50 test variations (10 per persona) was shown all 7 logos ONE AT A TIME in RANDOM ORDER. This methodology prevents comparative bias and measures isolated logo performance.

Sample Size: 50 total tests (5 personas Ã— 10 variations each)

Data Collection: For each logo individually, respondents rated trust (1-5), modernity (1-5), iGaming fit (1-5), and market perception (premium/mass-market/budget). After viewing all logos, respondents were asked to recall the most memorable ones.
