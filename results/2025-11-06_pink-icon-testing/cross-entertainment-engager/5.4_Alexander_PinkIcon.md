# User Research Interview: App Icon Design Concept
## Yes Games - Pink Background Icon Testing

**Persona:** Alexander (Variation 5.4 - Enthusiastic/Emotional Style)
**Age:** 24
**Location:** Odense, city center
**Date:** November 6, 2025
**Concept Tested:** Pink app icon with YES logotype

---

## Interview Questions & Responses

**Q1: What's your first reaction when you see a pink app icon on your iPhone home screen?**

YO! That's SICK! Pink is so bold! I love when brands have the confidence to do something different. This would absolutely pop on stream! The content potential is insane!

---

**Q2: How does pink make you feel in the context of a gaming/entertainment app?**

Pink feels FRESH, man! It's playful, it's modern, it's got personality. Everything else is so dark and serious. Pink is like, "Nah, we're here to have FUN and we're not afraid to show it!" That's fire!

Plus, pink is super on-trend right now. Streetwear, esports teams, content creators—everyone's using pink. It feels very 2025. Very now. I dig that energy!

---

**Q3: Would pink help you find and recognize the app quickly among your other apps?**

100%! Pink would be the FIRST thing I see. Instant recognition. Super convenient when I'm switching between apps while streaming or creating content. It'd be like a beacon on my screen!

---

**Q4: Does pink give you any specific associations or feelings (energy, fun, caution, cheap, premium, trust, etc.)?**

Pink gives me:
- **FUN energy!** (huge vibes)
- **Confidence and boldness** (takes guts to be pink!)
- **Modern and trendy** (very current)
- **Content-worthy** (super shareable, looks good on camera)
- **Playful and hype** (doesn't take itself too seriously)

It doesn't give me:
- **Hardcore gamer vibes** (but that's okay, not everything needs to be hardcore)
- **Super serious** (but gambling should be fun anyway!)

Honestly, pink feels like a brand that knows its vibe and owns it. That's what I respect! Don't be boring. Be bold!

---

**Q5: On a scale of 1-5, how much do you like the idea of a pink app icon? (1=hate it, 5=love it)**

**5 out of 5!**

LOVE IT! This is exactly the kind of bold move brands should be making! Pink is confident, it's different, it's memorable, and it's content-friendly. As someone who creates content around gaming, I can already imagine how good this would look on stream.

Pink has personality. It has energy. It says, "We're not afraid to be different." That's the kind of brand I want to work with and promote!

---

**Q6: Any other thoughts about pink as our brand color for the app icon?**

DO IT! Seriously, this is a no-brainer for me. The gaming and gambling space is SO boring. Everyone's copying each other with dark colors and serious vibes. Pink breaks the mold completely.

Here's why this is genius from a content creator perspective:

**1. It's visually striking.**
Pink looks AMAZING on camera. Whether I'm streaming on Twitch, making TikToks, or creating YouTube content, pink pops. It's eye-catching, it's memorable, it stands out in thumbnails. That's huge for shareability!

**2. It's conversation-worthy.**
People will talk about this. "Did you see that pink gambling app?" That's word-of-mouth marketing right there. Controversy and uniqueness drive engagement. Pink gives you both!

**3. It's modern and inclusive.**
Pink isn't just "for girls" anymore (if it ever was). Look at all the dudes rocking pink in streetwear, esports, music. Pink is for everyone who's confident. That's a powerful message!

**4. It fits the multi-vertical vibe.**
You've got sports, casino, esports—all different vibes. Pink is versatile enough to work across all of them. It's playful for casino, bold for sports, trendy for esports. It unifies them with a modern aesthetic!

**My advice:**
Commit to pink FULLY. Don't half-ass it. Make the app inside as bold and playful as the icon. Use pink throughout the UI (but tastefully—don't overwhelm). Create content around it. Lean into the boldness!

Pink could be your signature. "The pink gambling app." That's memorable! That's brandable! That's marketable!

I'm hyped about this! If Yes Games goes pink, I'd absolutely create content around it. It's different, it's bold, it's FUN. That's what content creators want to work with!

Let's GO!
