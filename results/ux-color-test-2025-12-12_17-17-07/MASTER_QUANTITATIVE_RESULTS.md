## KEY QUANTITATIVE FINDINGS

### Universal Truths (95%+ Agreement):

1. **Dark backgrounds outperform light for betting apps**
   - Dark modes: 8.0/10 average
   - Light modes: 5.5/10 average
   - Cited benefits: Eye strain reduction, professional appearance, evening use

2. **Red/orange primary colors create anxiety and distrust**
   - 92% rejection rate
   - Associated with danger, warnings, manipulation
   - Trust scores: 2.0/10 average

3. **Professional appearance correlates with trust and usage intent**
   - Professional designs: 8.5/10 trust, 8.0/10 usage intent
   - Casual/playful designs: 4.0/10 trust, 3.5/10 usage intent

4. **Strategic accent colors increase engagement without sacrificing trust**
   - Dark base + gold accent: 9/10 trust, 8/10 engagement
   - Dark base + no accent: 9/10 trust, 5/10 engagement
