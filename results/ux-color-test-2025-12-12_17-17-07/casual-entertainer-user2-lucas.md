## Comparative Questions

### Q13: Preferred Variants (Ranked)

**Top 3:**
1. **Variant 8** - This is the sweet spot for me. Professional enough that I trust it, exciting enough that I want to use it, and perfect for betting with friends during games. The color hits that perfect balance between fun and serious.

2. **Variant 7** - Really solid all-around. The teal-green feels both trustworthy and exciting. Would definitely use this comfortably. Great for social betting without any awkwardness.

3. **Variant 5** - The yellow is surprisingly awesome. Super fun and optimistic which matches the vibe of casual betting with friends. Makes the whole experience feel lighthearted and enjoyable.

**Bottom 3:**
1. **Variant 4** - Pink just doesn't work for me at all. Can't see myself using this seriously or showing it to my friends without getting roasted.

2. **Variant 9** - Way too flashy. Feels like it's trying too hard to be cool. The intense magenta is overwhelming and doesn't inspire trust.

3. **Variant 3** - Purple is interesting but just feels off for betting. Too sophisticated and removed from the sports betting vibe I want.

### Q14: Reasoning for Preferences

**Why Variant 8 wins:**
When I'm betting with friends during a game, I need something that:
- Looks legit enough that I trust it with my money (Variant 8 nails this)
- Feels exciting enough to match the energy of watching sports (definitely has this)
- Isn't embarrassing to pull out in front of my buddies (passes the bro test)
- Makes quick betting during commercial breaks easy (clear and organized)

Variant 8 just feels RIGHT. It's got energy but not chaos, trust but not boring corporate vibes, and it would look good on my phone screen when we're all hanging out watching the game. The color scheme says "this is professional but also fun" which is exactly what casual betting should be.

**Why Variant 5 is strong:**
The yellow is way better than expected. It's optimistic and fun which matches my attitude toward betting - I'm not trying to make a living, I'm trying to make the game more interesting. Yellow says "good times ahead" which is exactly the vibe.

**Why Variant 7 works:**
The teal-green is a safe bet (pun intended) but in a good way. It's familiar enough from other sports apps but distinctive enough to not feel generic. Would use this without hesitation.

**Why the bottom ones fail:**
- Variant 4's pink feels unserious and I'd be self-conscious using it
- Variant 9's magenta is sensory overload and feels unstable
- Variant 3's purple is too "fancy" for casual sports betting with the guys

At the end of the day, I'm a 26-year-old marketing guy betting for fun with friends. I need something that looks professional enough to trust but fun enough to enjoy. Variant 8 hits that perfect sweet spot where I'd confidently use it every game day, and Variants 5 and 7 are solid alternatives that I'd also be happy with.
