## Key Insights from Charlotte's Perspective

### What Works for This Persona:
1. **Bold, Unexpected Colors:** The pink and purple variants scored highest because they're unique and fashion-forward
2. **Luxury Aesthetics:** Premium, sophisticated color palettes that feel exclusive and high-end
3. **Instagram-Worthy Design:** Interfaces that would photograph well and be shareable on social media
4. **Modern, Trendy Choices:** Contemporary colors like coral, cyan, and gradient effects
5. **Visual Interest:** Depth, complexity, and sophistication in color application

### What Doesn't Work:
1. **Aggressive Colors:** Red was too anxiety-inducing and stressful
2. **Dated Palettes:** Green felt old-school and tacky
3. **Generic Choices:** Orange was too basic and forgettable
4. **Literal Interpretations:** Color schemes that are too obvious (green = money)
5. **Lack of Personality:** Anything that feels template-like or mass-market

### Charlotte's Color Preferences:
- **Loves:** Pink, Purple, Blue-Purple gradients, Cyan, Coral
- **Likes:** Yellow, Teal
- **Dislikes:** Orange (too basic)
- **Hates:** Red (too aggressive), Green (too dated)

### Design Priorities for This User Type:
1. Aesthetic appeal and visual sophistication
2. Unique, standout design that differentiates from competitors
3. Social media shareability
4. Luxury and premium feel
5. Modern, fashion-forward color choices
6. Emotional resonance (excitement, empowerment, exclusivity)

---

## Final Recommendation

For users like Charlotte - young, fashion-conscious, highly visual, and novelty-seeking - the **Pink Luxury System (Variant 7)** is the clear winner. It perfectly balances sophistication with playfulness, stands out from typical betting app aesthetics, and creates the kind of Instagram-worthy experience that this demographic craves.

The **Purple Royalty** and **Blue-Purple Gradient** systems are strong alternatives that also appeal to this user's desire for premium, aesthetically pleasing design.

Avoid aggressive colors (red), dated palettes (green), and generic choices (basic orange) that don't align with this persona's values of uniqueness, visual appeal, and fashion-forward thinking.
