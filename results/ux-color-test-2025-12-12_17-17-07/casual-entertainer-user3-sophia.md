## Overall Testing Summary

**Testing Persona:** Sophia (23, Casual Entertainer)
**Total Variants Tested:** 10
**Test Duration:** Full evaluation across all 14 questions
**Primary Device Context:** Mobile-first usage

### Key Preference Patterns:
- **Strong preference for:** Modern gradients, unique colors (pink, teal), visually striking designs
- **Moderate preference for:** Warm oranges, premium blacks, reds for excitement
- **Strong rejection of:** Overly bright colors (lime, yellow for extended use), boring corporate blues, anything too serious or outdated

### Trust vs. Excitement Balance:
Sophia shows an interesting split - she's drawn to fun, unique colors for excitement but recognizes that more professional colors build trust. Her ideal app balances both (like the blue/purple gradient).

### Social Shareability Factor:
Very important to this persona! Colors that are "Instagrammable" and conversation-starting rank higher, even if they sacrifice some functionality.

### Daily Use Considerations:
Despite loving bright, energetic colors initially, Sophia is realistic about eye strain and daily comfort - a key factor in her rankings.
