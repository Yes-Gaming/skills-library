## Recommendations

### Option 1: Proceed with Yellow (RISKY)
**Best for:** Targeting younger demographics (18-35), casual gamers, content-focused strategy

**Required Mitigations:**
1. **Compensatory trust measures:**
   - Prominent licensing badges on icon/splash screen
   - Immediate responsible gambling messaging
   - Dark blue or gray trust elements in UI

2. **Execution excellence:**
   - Sophisticated shade (golden-yellow vs neon)
   - Premium typography pairing
   - Consistent brand system throughout app

3. **Demographic acceptance:**
   - Accept potential loss of 40+ conservative bettors
   - Focus on volume vs lifetime value strategy

**Risk Level:** HIGH - May alienate highest-value demographic

---

### Option 2: Refined Yellow/Gold Hybrid (MODERATE RISK)
**Description:** Desaturated, golden-toned yellow with dark accents

**Advantages:**
- Maintains visibility benefits
- Adds premium/trust associations
- Reduces "caution" perception
- Better 40+ demographic appeal

**Implementation:**
- Gold-yellow background (#F4C430 range)
- Dark blue or charcoal "YES" typography
- Premium finish (gradient or texture)

**Risk Level:** MODERATE - Balanced approach

---

### Option 3: Alternative Strategy (CONSERVATIVE)
**Description:** Dark/neutral primary with yellow accents

**Examples:**
- Dark navy background with yellow "YES"
- Charcoal with yellow highlight elements
- Deep teal with golden accents

**Advantages:**
- Maintains some yellow differentiation
- Addresses trust concerns directly
- Signals professionalism and security
- Appeals to all demographics

**Risk Level:** LOW - Safe positioning
