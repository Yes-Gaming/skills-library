## Executive Summary

### Test Overview

We conducted isolated interviews with 25 synthetic personas across 5 demographic segments to evaluate the proposed yellow app icon for Yes Games. Each persona was interviewed independently to prevent context pollution and ensure authentic responses.

### Overall Rating: **3.4/5**

The yellow app icon concept receives **moderate approval** with significant demographic variations. While younger users (18-35) respond positively, the **critical 40+ Trust Seeker demographic shows concerning skepticism**.

### Key Insight

**Yellow delivers exceptional visibility but at a potential cost to trust perception, particularly among high-lifetime-value users aged 40+.**

## Key Findings

### Finding #1: Visibility vs Trust Trade-off

**The Data:**
- **Visibility Score:** 10/10 (100% agreement)
- **Trust Score:** 3/10 (only 20% associate yellow with trust)

**Implication:** You gain immediate attention but may lose credibility with high-value users.

---

### Finding #2: Age Correlation

**Ratings by Age Group:**
- **18-30 years:** 4.1/5 average (enthusiastic support)
- **30-40 years:** 3.4/5 average (mixed feelings)
- **40+ years:** 2.5/5 average (skeptical to negative)

**Implication:** Yellow's appeal decreases sharply with age, inversely correlated with typical lifetime value.

---

### Finding #3: Communication Style Matters

**Ratings by Response Style:**
- **Enthusiastic/Emotional:** 4.9/5 average
- **Brief/Direct:** 3.3/5 average
- **Skeptical/Critical:** 2.3/5 average
- **Analytical/Comparative:** 3.5/5 average

**Implication:** Critical thinkers and skeptics strongly dislike yellow; emotional/enthusiastic users love it.

---

### Finding #4: Context Execution Critical

**Common Theme Across 80% of Responses:**
"The success of yellow depends entirely on execution quality."

**Specific Requirements Mentioned:**
- Premium shade selection (not neon or muted)
- Sophisticated typography pairing
- Consistent brand system throughout app
- Visible trust signals (licensing, responsible gambling)
- Dark/neutral trust elements in UI

**Implication:** Yellow is high-risk, high-reward - requires flawless execution.

2. **Trust Signal Integration**
   - Spillemyndigheden licensing badge prominent
   - Responsible gambling messaging immediate

   - Age verification signals clear

   - Strong contrast for "YES" logotype

   - Test across device sizes

4. **Brand System Coherence**
   - Yellow must appear consistently in app
