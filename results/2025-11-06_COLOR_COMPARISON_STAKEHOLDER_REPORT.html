Research Methodology

Standardized Interview Questions

All 225 persona interviews followed an identical 6-question framework to ensure consistent methodology across all 9 colors:

  1. Q1: First Reaction - "What's your first reaction when you see a [COLOR] app icon on your iPhone home screen?"
  2. Q2: Emotional Context - "How does [COLOR] make you feel in the context of a gaming/entertainment app?"
  3. Q3: Recognition & Findability - "Would [COLOR] help you find and recognize the app quickly among your other apps?"
  4. Q4: Associations - "Does [COLOR] give you any specific associations or feelings (energy, fun, caution, cheap, premium, etc.)?"
  5. Q5: Rating Scale - "On a scale of 1-5, how much do you like the idea of a [COLOR] app icon? (1=hate it, 5=love it)"
  6. Q6: Additional Thoughts - "Any other thoughts about [COLOR] as our brand color for the app icon?"

Testing approach: Each of the 225 interviews was conducted by an isolated agent instance to prevent context pollution and ensure authentic, independent responses. Questions replaced [COLOR] with the specific color being tested (e.g., "yellow", "purple", "red").
