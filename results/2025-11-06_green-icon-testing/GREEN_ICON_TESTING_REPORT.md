## Executive Summary

### Test Overview

We conducted isolated interviews with 25 synthetic personas across 5 demographic segments to evaluate the proposed green app icon for Yes Games. Each persona was interviewed independently using the exact methodology from yellow/red icon testing.

### Overall Rating: **3.5/5**

The green app icon concept receives **moderate to positive approval** with SIGNIFICANTLY BETTER performance among Trust Seekers (40+ demographic) compared to yellow.

### Key Insight

**Green delivers strong trust perception and differentiation, performing exceptionally well with high-value Trust Seeker demographic while maintaining reasonable appeal across other segments.**
