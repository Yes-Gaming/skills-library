# User Research Interview: App Icon Design Concept
## Yes Games - RED Background Icon Testing

**Persona:** Mikkel (Variation 1.4 - Enthusiastic/Emotional Style)
**Age:** 22
**Location:** Odense, city center
**Date:** November 6, 2025
**Concept Tested:** RED app icon with YES logotype

---

## Interview Questions & Responses

**Q1: What's your first reaction when you see a RED app icon on your home screen?**

WHOA! That's INTENSE! Red is like... BAM! Right in your face! I love bold stuff!

---

**Q2: How does RED make you feel in the context of a gaming/entertainment app?**

It's EXCITING! Like pure adrenaline! Red gets your blood pumping, you know? It's the color of passion, of GO GO GO! Perfect for late-night gaming sessions after work! It matches that RUSH feeling!

---

**Q3: Would RED help you find and recognize the app quickly among your other apps?**

100%! You can't miss red! It's like a beacon on my screen! When I want to jump in and play, BOOM—there it is! No searching!

---

**Q4: Does RED give you any specific associations or feelings (energy, fun, caution, cheap, premium, etc.)?**

Energy, power, excitement, thrill! It's like the color of winning! Of hitting a jackpot! Of scoring big! I don't get cheap vibes from it—I get BOLD, CONFIDENT, FEARLESS vibes! Love it!

---

**Q5: On a scale of 1-5, how much do you like the idea of a RED app icon? (1=hate it, 5=love it)**

5! LOVE IT! It's bold, it's fearless, it matches the excitement I feel when I play! Red is passion! It's perfect!

---

**Q6: Any other thoughts about RED as our brand color for the app icon?**

Red is FIRE! It's the color of champions! Of winners! Of people who go ALL IN! If you want to stand out and make a statement, red is IT! Don't be boring—be BOLD!
