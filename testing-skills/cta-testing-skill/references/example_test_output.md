# Example Test Output - CTA Testing

This is a sample of what a completed CTA test report looks like.

## Test Configuration
- **Persona tested:** The Casual Entertainer (Age 24, Copenhagen)
- **Context:** Landing page hero - New visitor from Instagram ad
- **Device:** Mobile (iPhone)
- **Rounds per persona:** 10
- **CTAs tested:**
  - CTA A: "Start Playing Free"
  - CTA B: "Join Now"
  - CTA C: "Claim Your Bonus"
  - CTA D: "Get Started"
- **Date conducted:** November 3, 2025

## Quantitative Results

### Q1: Action Intent (Which would you most likely click?)
The Casual Entertainer:
- **CTA A ("Start Playing Free")**: 6/10 (60%)
- CTA B ("Join Now"): 2/10 (20%)
- CTA C ("Claim Your Bonus"): 2/10 (20%)
- CTA D ("Get Started"): 0/10 (0%)

### Q4: Value Perception (Which creates strongest urgency/value?)
The Casual Entertainer:
- **CTA C ("Claim Your Bonus")**: 7/10 (70%)
- CTA A ("Start Playing Free"): 2/10 (20%)
- CTA B ("Join Now"): 1/10 (10%)
- CTA D ("Get Started"): 0/10 (0%)

### Intent-to-Value Gap Analysis
**Critical Finding:** 40% gap between what drives clicks vs. what creates value perception
- **Preference winner:** "Start Playing Free" (60%)
- **Value winner:** "Claim Your Bonus" (70%)
- **Gap:** Different CTAs win different metrics

### Order Bias Analysis
No significant order bias detected across presentation positions.

## Qualitative Insights

### Why "Start Playing Free" Won for Clicks (Q1)

**Key Themes:**
1. **Zero Friction** - "Free means no commitment, I can just try it"
2. **Instant Action** - "Start Playing" = immediate gratification
3. **Clarity** - "I know exactly what happens when I tap it"
4. **Trust** - "Free feels safer than bonus (no strings attached)"

**Representative Quotes:**
- "I like that it says 'free' - means I don't have to deposit right away"
- "'Start Playing' tells me I can jump straight into games"
- "Clear and honest, not trying to trick me with bonus terms"
- "Free = low risk, I'll tap that for sure"

### Why "Claim Your Bonus" Won for Value (Q4)

**Key Themes:**
1. **FOMO** - "Bonus makes it feel like I'm getting something special"
2. **Urgency** - "'Claim' suggests it might disappear"
3. **Personalization** - "'Your' bonus feels targeted to me"
4. **Value Signal** - "Bonus = more value than just free play"

**Representative Quotes:**
- "Makes me feel like there's actual value waiting for me"
- "I want to know what the bonus is - creates curiosity"
- "'Claim' makes it feel exclusive and time-sensitive"
- "Sounds like I'm getting extra, not just the basic experience"

### Why "Join Now" Underperformed

**Friction Points:**
- "Too generic, every site says this"
- "Sounds corporate and boring"
- "Doesn't tell me what I'm joining or why"
- "'Now' feels pushy, like they're desperate"
- "Feels like commitment without value proposition"

### Why "Get Started" Failed

**Friction Points:**
- "Vague - get started with what?"
- "Sounds like work, not fun"
- "Too formal for a gambling site"
- "Zero personality or excitement"
- "Feels like software onboarding, not entertainment"

## Friction Coefficient Analysis

### Low Friction (Would Click Easily)
- **"Start Playing Free"** - Minimal hesitation, clear action, zero commitment
- **"Claim Your Bonus"** - Creates pull, but some hesitation about bonus terms

### Medium Friction
- **"Join Now"** - Generic, requires mental effort to process value

### High Friction (Likely to Bounce)
- **"Get Started"** - Sounds like effort, unclear benefit, boring

## Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation: A/B Test Strategy

**For Maximum Clicks (Top of Funnel):**
Use **"Start Playing Free"**
- 60% preference rate shows strong click intent
- Removes friction of commitment
- Clear value proposition
- Appeals to entertainment motivation

**For Maximum Conversions (After Click):**
Use **"Claim Your Bonus"** as secondary CTA after registration
- 70% value perception rating
- Creates urgency and exclusivity
- Better for users who've shown initial interest
- Leverage FOMO for deposit conversion

### Intent-to-Value Gap Strategy

**The Gap is Your Opportunity:**
- **Landing Page Hero:** "Start Playing Free" (gets them in)
- **Post-Registration:** "Claim Your Bonus" (converts them)
- **Email Re-engagement:** "Claim Your Bonus" (brings them back)

This two-CTA strategy maximizes both traffic AND conversion.

### Context-Specific Recommendations

**Mobile Landing Page (New Visitors):**
- Primary: "Start Playing Free"
- Why: Mobile users want instant gratification, zero friction
- Expected uplift: +25-40% vs. generic CTAs

**Registration Completion Screen:**
- Primary: "Claim Your Welcome Bonus"
- Why: User has committed, now maximize value perception
- Expected uplift: +30-50% deposit rate

**Abandoned Cart Email:**
- Primary: "Claim Your Bonus (24h Left)"
- Why: Urgency + value + FOMO
- Expected uplift: +15-25% recovery rate

### What to Avoid

❌ **"Join Now"** - Too generic, no differentiation
❌ **"Get Started"** - Sounds like work, kills entertainment vibe
❌ Using same CTA across all contexts - Different stages need different messaging

## Key Learnings

### What Drives Clicks ≠ What Drives Value
This test reveals the critical difference between:
- **Initial action** (what gets them to click)
- **Perceived value** (what makes them feel they're getting something worthwhile)

**Implication:** Use different CTAs at different funnel stages.

### "Free" Beats "Bonus" for Cold Traffic
For users who don't know your brand:
- Free = Safe, low commitment, easy to try
- Bonus = Might have terms, requires trust

**Implication:** Lead with free, convert with bonuses.

### Specificity Matters
Vague CTAs ("Get Started", "Join Now") performed worst because they:
- Don't communicate clear value
- Sound like every other site
- Create decision paralysis

**Implication:** Be specific about what happens when they click.

### Entertainment Beats Effort
For gambling/entertainment platforms:
- "Start Playing" > "Get Started"
- "Claim" > "Join"
- Fun language > Corporate language

**Implication:** Match CTA tone to product category.
